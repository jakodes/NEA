Jackal's early years on this Earth are unclear due to incomplete foster care records. Eventually released into the care of his older brother, the two of them went underground from official records until their presence was re-detected several years later due to his brother's unsolved murder. Being guided by a sympathetic rookie police officer with an unwavering sense of justice, he grew up with the ideals that makes a strong soldier and righteous man. The day he turned eighteen, he joined the Cuerpo corps with the use of his exceptional lateral thinking and to this day conducts further training to perfect his craft.
